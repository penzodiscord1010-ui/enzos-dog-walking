❓ FAQ

Common questions — answered!

🌧️ What if it rains?
A little rain won't stop us! I'll still do the walk unless there's lightning or a serious storm. I'll always text you ahead of time if I need to reschedule due to weather.
🐕‍🦺 Do you walk multiple dogs at once?
I walk up to 2 dogs at a time — either 2 of your own dogs, or 1 dog per booking. I don't mix dogs from different families on the same walk for safety reasons.
💵 How do I pay?
I accept Cash, Cash App, PayPal, and Venmo. Payment is due after the walk. You can choose your preferred method when booking!
📍 Where exactly do you walk?
I stick to the Hillsdale neighborhood — sidewalks, local streets, and nearby trails. I know the area really well since I live here and walk my own 3 dogs here all the time!
⏰ How far in advance should I book?
At least 24 hours in advance is ideal so I can plan my schedule. Last-minute requests are okay but not guaranteed — reach out and I'll let you know!
🔑 How do I give you access to my dog?
Most clients just leave their dog with a family member or leave a key/gate code in the booking notes. We'll figure out the easiest option for you when I confirm the booking!
